1. **Unused Tables/Columns (DB-02):**

   - Identify any FK relationship where the cascade behavior could leave orphaned records (e.g., `.OnDelete(DeleteBehavior.SetNull)` or no cascade on required relationships).

     - CoachCoacheeMappings where CoachId or CoacheeId not in AspNetUsers
     - ProtonDeliverableProgresses where AssignmentId not in ProtonTrackAssignments

     - PackageUserResponses where AssignmentId not in UserPackageAssignments
     - IdpItems where UserId not in AspNetUsers
     - CoachingLogs where UserId not in AspNetUsers
     - Notifications/UserNotifications where UserId not in AspNetUsers

3. Document all findings. For unused tables/columns, recommend removal or mark as "retained for future use" with justification. For orphaned records, document the query and count.
