### Cross-cutting Pattern 3: Cache Invalidation Co-located with Mutation

**Source:** `Controllers/AssessmentAdminController.cs:3234, :3314`, `Controllers/AdminController.cs:104`
**Apply to:** D-03 (3 actions: `AddCategory` L320, `EditCategory` L372, `DeleteCategory` L401)

**Konvensi:**
- Invalidation **inline** di action body (no helper extraction untuk single key) — codebase precedent
- **Setelah** SaveChangesAsync (kalau save gagal & throw, cache tidak di-invalidate prematurely)
- **Sebelum** RedirectToAction/return (next request akan lihat fresh cache)
