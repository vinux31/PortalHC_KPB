  `once` modifier sticky internally — re-set hx-trigger attribute tidak reset internalData.triggeredOnce
  state. Akibatnya: setelah klik Assessment lalu balik ke Training (klik 2nd), Training wrapper
  STUCK di skeleton "Memuat data" — trigger tidak fire ulang, content gone.

  childrenCount=1 (skeleton only, no table).

    cause: "Handler listens to htmx:afterSwap event yang fire untuk semua swap"
    fix_hint: "Listen ke filter form change event saja (#filter-form input/select), atau check evt.detail.elt apakah filter form vs tab wrapper"
  - id: BUG-2B
    name: "Restored hx-trigger after invalidation doesn't fire (once modifier sticky)"
    cause: "HTMX track once state internally di internalData.triggeredOnce; setAttribute + htmx.process tidak reset"
    fix_hint: "Pakai htmx.cleanup(wrapper) dulu sebelum setAttribute + process, atau remove `once` modifier dari restored hx-trigger (akan re-fire setiap shown.bs.tab event)"
