3 Platform Terpadu

CMP · CDP · BP — manajemen, pengembangan, & strategic partner

SLIDE 5 / 23
📋
CMP
Competency Management Platform
Platform digital untuk pengelolaan kompetensi terintegrasi — penyusunan KKJ, IDP, asesmen teknis & Safety.
  • 📊 Assessment — Ujian online & sertifikasi
  • 🎓 Assessment Proton — Program 3-tahun
  • 🔄 Pre / Post Test — Ukur efektivitas training
  • 🏆 Sertifikasi — Otomatis + renewal lifecycle
🌱
CDP
Competency Development Platform
Pembelajaran terstruktur untuk menutup gap kompetensi — prinsip blended Learning (Assignment, Coaching, Self Study).
  • 🎯 Coaching Proton — Silabus + deliverable + review multi-role
  • 📋 IDP — Individual Development Plan tahunan
  • 📚 Training Records — Riwayat training + sertifikat
🚧
BP
Business Partner
Modul HRBP — strategic partner antara HC & unit operasional untuk workforce planning, employee relations, & advisory.
  • For Future — in roadmap
